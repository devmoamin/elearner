Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers to help guide you through your learning journey on E-Learner.

What courses are available on E-Learner?

E-Learner offers a variety of courses across different categories such as programming, design, business, and personal development. You can browse and filter courses based on your interests and skill level.

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, simply create an account or log in to your existing account, browse our course catalog, and click on the 'Enroll Now' button for the course you're interested in. You’ll be granted immediate access upon completing your enrollment.

Can I get a refund if I’m not satisfied with a course?

Yes, we offer a refund policy. If you are not satisfied with a course, you can request a refund within 7 days of enrollment, provided that no content has been accessed. Please refer to our Refund Policy for more details.

Do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes! Upon successfully completing a course, you will receive a certificate that you can add to your profile and share with potential employers or colleagues.

How long do I have access to the course materials?

You will have lifetime access to the course materials after enrolling. This means you can go back and review the course at any time, even after completion.

How do I contact support if I have an issue?

If you encounter any issues or have questions, you can reach our support team by emailing us at support@elearner.com or by visiting our Contact Us page.

Are there any discounts available for courses?

From time to time, we offer discounts and promotions on certain courses. To stay updated on our latest offers, sign up for our newsletter or follow us on social media.

Can I get a bulk discount for group enrollments?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk or group enrollments. Please contact our support team at support@elearner.com to learn more about bulk discounts for your organization or team.
